The method

EXPLAIN → DEMONSTRATE → PRACTICE → REVIEW

Every module runs this exact loop. No shortcuts. Each concept is grounded in a real dataset you'll grow familiar with.

STEP 01

Explain

Plain English first. The idea behind the syntax. Two or three sentences max.

STEP 02

Demonstrate

A working query on a real table. See the input, see the output. No abstractions.

STEP 03

Practice

One focused exercise. You write the query. SQLSENSEI checks it against the expected result.

STEP 04

Review

Three key takeaways. Then a checkpoint quiz at the end of every level.
